Introduction

With surveillance powers of the Indian government and its agencies continuously expanding through legal and extra­legal initiatives, secure communications and strong protections of the right to privacy ­ currently absent in India ­ have become all the more important. However, activists have generally struggled to get this message across to broader audiences effectively, including to women human rights defenders, and support for their calls for stronger privacy protections and other measures is growing only slowly
